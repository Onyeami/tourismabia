Our Services

Welcome to Onye-Ije Tourism Company, where we offer a range of exciting and memorable services to make your visit to Abia State an unforgettable experience.

Our Main Focus is our tourist satisfaction

Managed by highly qualified and experienced tour guides

We intend to make the travel experience of every person who seeks Onye-Ije Tourism Company a very memorable one.

Discover the rich cultural heritage of Abia State with our guided heritage tours. Visit ancient landmarks, traditional villages, and immerse yourself in the local customs and traditions.

Explore the breathtaking natural beauty of Abia State with our nature excursions. Hike through Ohafia forests, experience the blue colour of Azumini-Blue river, and encounter diverse wildlife in their natural habitats.

Taste the authentic flavours of Abia State with our culinary adventures. Indulge in traditional dishes and savor the unique blend of local ingredients and spices.

Engage in creative art and craft workshops. See Akwete traditional cloth weaving and get some for your souvenirs to take home.

Showcasing the rich cultural heritage of Abia State.

Ready to embark on an unforgettable journey through Abia State? Get in touch with us now to book your tour or to inquire about our services.
